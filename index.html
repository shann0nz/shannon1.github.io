WILD WILD WET

Have a splashing fun time at Singapore's biggest waterpark!
Rides ranging from kids-friendly to moderate to extreme!




Rides & Attractions

• Free Fall: Asia's Longest Water Slides

Ride rating: Extreme
Take the plunge down a near-vertical drop at speeds of up to
50 feet per second on one of Asia's longest free fall water slides.


• Royal Flush Water Rafting Tunnel Slide

Ride rating: Moderate
Asia's first hybrid ride featuring both the Behemoth BOWL40 and TornadoWAVE rides in one!
Sit in a group of four on a raft on this splashing water slide, as you drop into the enormous
bowl and slide down the curves at manic speeds, swooshing up a wall and down a tunnel.
This ride is also a great choice for a family raft ride.

• Kidz Zone Water Theme Park for Children

Ride rating: Kids-friendly
Let the kids enjoy a pint-sized versions of the big rides such as
Royal Flush and Kraken Racers, designed to provide maximum entertainment to the little ones.
Suitable for toddlers and children of all ages to frolic around in a safe and fun environment!
Children must be supervised by an adult at all times.
